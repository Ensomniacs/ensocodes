A Special Thank You

When Season of the Plague first made it's way into the twitter universe, I honestly did not know what to expect. This project started off as a small group of 8-9 very talented artists all whom have a drive and desire to work in the gaming industry. What originally started off as a small joke, toes in the water jump for myself has turned into such a wonderful journey over the last month and a half since joining Arripe Gladium.

First and foremost, I want to thank Jay, our founder. He's poured his heart and soul into this project to bring it to life. I want to say thank you to all of our project leads: Moira, Reed, Drexis, Parisito, Protheon, Wal, and Breshi for bringin the group together. Your leadership and directive has been amazing to watch unfold and help lead the group from the origin of SotP to the execution of the ARG and bringing life to Season of the Plague.

Thank you to our design team for giving it their all in every piece individually and as a team. Everyone's flexibility and feed back with each step of the process not only helped us build skills to work flexibily with one another but form bonds of friendship through this journey. Thank you to our content creators: Hush, AscendantNomad, Vet, and Byf for their contribution to the project from hyping up the community to consulting in project execution.

And last, but not least... Thank you to the community.

Thank you to everyone who not only supported our team up to our release and beyond. Thank you to the community for sharing this passion not just for SIVA but for Destiny as a whole. We hope that you will stay with us as we continue on our journey into the future.

With love and light. See you star side, guardians.
